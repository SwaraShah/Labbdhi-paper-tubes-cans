About Us

Established in 2002, Labbdhi Paper Tubes Pvt. Ltd. has made a name for itself in the list of top service providers of Paper Cons, Paper Tubes and Paper Cans in India.

We have earned a reputation with our customers as a reliable and dependable supplier. Making a paper tube is a science and an art. It requires high quality raw materials, attention to detail during the manufacturing process, trained personnel, and a quality control system that ensures the end product is made according to order specifications.
